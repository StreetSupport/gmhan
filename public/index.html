Greater Manchester Food Security Action Network - Ending Food Poverty in Greater Manchester

The Greater Manchester Food Security Action Network (GMFSAN) brings together everyone in Greater Manchester working to end the scandal of food poverty:

People with personal insights into food insecurity, people from the frontline organisations, public sector officers, and anyone working to find more effective solutions to the food poverty crisis.
